Mycelium compost, cover crops, and agroforestry windbreaks lifted grain yields by 2x while reducing inputs. ZenTrust agronomists co-designed training with local cooperatives, focusing on soil biology instead of chemical dependence.

Profits from the bumper harvests fund school lunches and seed banks, proving regeneration is both climate and community finance.
